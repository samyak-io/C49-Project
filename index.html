intersting facts about camels:

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyelids
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
  3. Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

an origami chameleon

intersting facts about chameleons:

  1. There are around 160 species of chameleon
  2. The chameleons' eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs
    so they can see two different objects at the same time
  3. Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings

an origami cicada

intersting facts about cicadas:

  1. There are about 3000 cicada species
  2. Though a group of these insects are called a cloud or plague, cicadas usually don't
    cause the harm that locusts cause to crops and local vegetation.
  3. Some cicada calls can be heard up to 1 mile (1.5 kilometer) away, according to National Geographic
